The itinerary

6 days, city by city.

Every day is planned so you get the best of both cities while still having time to enjoy the experience properly.

Day 01
Arrive in Amsterdam

Land at Schiphol and transfer to the hotel. Meet the group over your first dinner together. The city lights up beautifully at dusk, with canals, bikes and that calm Amsterdam mood setting the tone.

Arrival Hotel check in Group dinner
Day 02
Amsterdam city tour and Keukenhof or Zaanse Schans

Explore Dam Square, the Royal Palace and the Anne Frank House exterior. Before 12 May, enjoy Keukenhof Gardens. After 13 May, visit Zaanse Schans and a Dutch cheese farm. End the day with a scenic canal cruise.

Keukenhof Canal cruise Dutch culture
Day 03
Brussels stop and arrive in Paris

Travel from Amsterdam to Paris with a stop in Brussels. Visit Grand Place, Manneken Pis and the Atomium area. Then continue to Paris, check in, refresh and step into the city that feels cinematic in every way.

Brussels Grand Place Paris arrival
Day 04
Paris highlights

Enjoy a full day in Paris with a guided city tour covering Place Vendôme, Opéra Garnier, Champs Élysées and Arc de Triomphe. Then head up to the second level of the Eiffel Tower and finish with a Seine River cruise.

Eiffel Tower Seine cruise City tour
Day 05
Disneyland Paris

Spend the day at Disneyland Paris. Whether it is classic Disneyland Park or Walt Disney Studios, the day is full of fun, energy and content worthy moments with the group.

Disneyland Full day Group experience
Day 06
Fly home from Paris

Enjoy your final breakfast in Paris, check out and head to the airport. You leave with the kind of memories that stay with you long after the flight home.

Departure Last breakfast
